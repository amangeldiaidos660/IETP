NIETF was established on April 10, 1992 by the order of the Kazakh State National University in accordance with the resolution No. 295 of the Council of Ministers of the Kazakh SSR dated July 24, 1990.

On May 27, 2003, in accordance with the resolution of the Government of the Republic of Kazakhstan No. 37 dated January 17, 2003, NIIEFF was reorganized as a subsidiary state enterprise of the Kazakh National University on May 27, 2003. Al-Farabi.
MAIN RESEARCH AREAS OF THE INSTITUTE
physics of gases, plasmas and liquids; physics of non-equilibrium processes in plasma and plasma-like environment;
problems of thermal physics and thermal energy, research of turbulence and combustion processes;
problems of condensed state physics and materials science, creation of technology for obtaining materials with required properties; electrophysical, optical and structural studies of matter;
interaction of nuclear, electromagnetic radiation and accelerated particles with matter; nuclear, thermonuclear energy, radiation safety;
theoretical and experimental nuclear physics; physics of cosmic rays;
radiophysics and physical electronics;
actual problems of the theory of relativity, gravitation and quantum chromodynamics;
problems of evolution of open systems.

NII management timeline
1992-1993 The first director of the ETF research institute is the former rector of KazNU. al-Farabi, Honored Academician of the National Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Doctor of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, Professor Kozhamkulov Tolegen Abdisagiuly, at that time the dean of the Faculty of Physics of KazMU, on whose initiative the institute was established. .

In 1993, the acting director was the corresponding member of the National Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Doctor of Physics and Mathematics, Professor Baiimbetov Fazilkhan Baiimbetov.

From the middle of 1993 to 1995, Andrey Stepanovich Drobyshev, Doctor of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, was the director of the Scientific Research Institute of ETP.

From February 1996 to April 2000, the institute was headed by Doctor of Physics and Mathematics, Professor Tazhibayeva Irina Lashkarovna.

Associate Professor Ibraev Baurzhan Mukhtarkhanuly served as director in April-June 2000.

From June 2000 to November 2006, the institute was headed by Doctor of Physics and Mathematics, Professor Ramazanov Tilekkabyl Sabituly.

From November 2006 to the present, the institute was headed by Oleg Aleksandrovich Lavrishchev, candidate of physical and mathematical sciences, associate professor.
